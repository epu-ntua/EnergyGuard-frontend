Antrodoco Renewable Energy Community

Organization: ENGREEN Location: Antrodoco, Italy Domain: Renewable Energy Communities

The Antrodoco REC Node provides access to a real Renewable Energy Community located in the Lazio Region of Italy. It brings together community and technical data, including member and asset profiles, photovoltaic plants, battery storage systems, real-time monitoring data from producers and prosumers, selected consumer-side measurements, historical hourly records of energy withdrawal and injection, and weather data.

Within the node, the Antrodoco REC Digital Twin acts as the modelling and simulation layer. It turns these data into an interactive representation of how the community works: how energy is produced, consumed, stored and shared. Users can explore the REC structure, visualise members and assets, monitor key energy indicators, analyse production and consumption trends, estimate shared energy and related benefits, and compare current REC behaviour with forecasted, simulated or AI-enhanced scenarios.

The node supports four TEF services:
  • Smart Energy Optimiser
    Helps users identify opportunities to improve REC operation by analysing production, consumption, self-consumption, battery use and grid exchanges.
  • Fair Dynamic Pricing
    Supports the assessment of shared energy, expected incentives and the economic effects of different consumption, pricing or behavioural scenarios.
  • Multi-Source Planning Suite
    Allows users to evaluate future REC configurations, such as new photovoltaic plants, additional storage capacity, new members or alternative operating strategies.
  • Predictive Maintenance Monitoring
    Helps detect unusual operating patterns in monitored photovoltaic assets and supports the interpretation of possible technical, communication, shading, degradation or battery-related issues.
