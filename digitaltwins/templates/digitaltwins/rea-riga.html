Riga's Multi-Apartment Residential Buildings

Organization: Riga Energy Agency, State Construction Control Bureau of Latvia Location: Riga, Latvia Domain: Buildings

This node represents the large-scale residential building stock of the city of Riga, mainly composed of multi-apartment buildings from different construction periods and with diverse building types and energy performance characteristics. It provides a real urban environment for evaluating AI-driven energy efficiency solutions at building and city scale, supported by data from the national EPC registry, Latvia's Construction Information Systems, municipal geospatial databases, and monitored heating consumption records.

Within the node, the Digital Twin of Riga's Buildings acts as the modelling and visualization layer. It presents a geospatial data-enriched view of the city's residential building stock through an interactive map interface. Each building is enriched with EPC information, structural and audit data, continuously updated heating consumption records and city-level historical and real-time meteorological data. Users can explore, filter, and analyse building performance within the urban context.

Beyond visual exploration, API access to the underlying datasets enables solution providers to integrate building data into their applications, analytics pipelines and decision support tools, supporting AI experimentation and validation in the building sector.

Use cases:

Building energy performance analysis, Retrofit planning and renovation scenario simulation, Energy consumption savings estimation.
