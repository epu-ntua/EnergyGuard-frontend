BER Hydrogen Testing Facility

Physical PEM Electrolyzer Testing Environment

The BER node provides a real-world testing environment for PEM electrolyzers, enabling controlled hydrogen production experiments and the generation of high-quality operational data.

Users can request custom experimental campaigns through the EnergyGuard platform, defining operating conditions such as load profiles and system behavior. The resulting datasets support the development and validation of advanced analytics and AI-based solutions for hydrogen systems, including performance optimization, predictive maintenance, and operational strategy assessment.

By combining physical assets with structured experimentation, the BER node offers reliable, data-driven insights derived from actual system operation, supporting the transition toward efficient and scalable hydrogen technologies.
