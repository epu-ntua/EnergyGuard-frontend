CARTIF Hydrogen Node

Hybrid hydrogen-electric-thermal energy system

The CARTIF Hydrogen Node provides a virtual testing environment for hydrogen and renewable energy systems. It allows users to safely explore, evaluate, and optimize energy management strategies without affecting real equipment.

The service is based on a real hybrid energy facility that combines hydrogen production, hydrogen storage, batteries, thermal storage, and heat recovery technologies. Through a Digital Twin of the installation, users can simulate different operating conditions, assess system performance, and test AI-driven control strategies before deploying them in real-world environments.

The platform helps users understand how renewable energy, hydrogen technologies, and energy storage can work together to improve efficiency, flexibility, and sustainability. It supports scenario analysis, performance benchmarking, and the evaluation of advanced AI solutions for forecasting, optimization, and energy management.

Key benefits include:
  • Safe experimentation without risks to physical assets.
  • Evaluation of energy and hydrogen management strategies before deployment.
  • Comparison of alternative operating scenarios and control approaches.
  • Access to performance indicators related to efficiency, renewable energy utilization, flexibility, and hydrogen storage.
  • Validation of AI-based energy management solutions in a realistic environment.

The CARTIF Hydrogen Node is particularly valuable for researchers, AI developers, energy technology providers, utilities, and industrial users seeking to accelerate innovation and reduce the risks associated with deploying advanced energy solutions.
