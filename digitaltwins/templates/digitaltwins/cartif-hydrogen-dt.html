Run the CARTIF Hydrogen Digital Twin to predict the behaviour of the hybrid hydrogen-electric-thermal system over a 24-hour window, based on the site location and the sizing of its energy assets.

Configure a simulation
One run, a full system prediction

The Digital Twin predicts the coordinated operation of the electrolyzer, hydrogen storage, batteries and heat pump over the simulation window. If real facility measurements are provided for the same period, the platform also reports how closely the prediction matches them.

How it works

Configure

Set the site location and asset sizing, or use CARTIF's default configuration.

Provide real values

Optionally upload measured facility data for the same period to enable AI accuracy comparison.

Run

The Digital Twin runs the predictive models over a 24-hour window.

Results

Review predicted energy flows and, where supplied, prediction accuracy.

What you'll need to provide

Site location

Latitude, longitude, altitude and time zone, used to determine the weather and climate conditions applied to the simulation.

Energy asset sizing

Power and capacity ratings of the electrolyzer, hydrogen storage, batteries and heat pump.

Real facility values (optional)

Measured data for the same period, used to compute AI-prediction accuracy metrics.

Configuration

1. Site Location

Determines the weather and climate conditions used in the simulation. Defaults to the CARTIF facility in Valladolid.
