SOEC Digital Twin Simulation Platform

Organization: CEA Location: France Domain: Hydrogen

Access and interact with a comprehensive digital twin of a SOEC system through an intuitive and user-friendly interface. The platform enables users to create, customize, and execute simulation scenarios by adjusting key system parameters and operating conditions.

Using the simulation workspace, users can:
  • Configure and compare multiple energy system scenarios.
  • Modify technical, operational, and environmental parameters.
  • Launch simulations directly from the platform.
  • Monitor simulation progress in real time.
  • Access and download raw simulation outputs for further analysis.
  • Explore detailed performance indicators and system behavior under different assumptions.

Designed for engineers, analysts, researchers, and decision-makers, the platform provides direct access to the underlying digital twin, enabling data-driven evaluation of energy strategies, operational optimization, and what-if analyses in a secure and scalable environment.

Use cases:

Scenario generation with AI tool, Scenario simulation and AI algorithm testing, Experimental validation of selected scenarios, EHT module's state of health diagnostic tool
