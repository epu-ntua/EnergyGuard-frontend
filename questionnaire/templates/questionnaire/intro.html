This questionnaire helps organisations in the energy sector assess their obligations under the EU Artificial Intelligence Act (AI Act). It will guide you through a short assessment to determine your role (such as provider or deployer), the risk category that applies to your AI system or general-purpose AI model, and the specific obligations that follow from that classification.

Note that some roles carry no obligations under the AI Act and therefore fall outside the scope of this questionnaire.

What to expect:
  • Duration: 10-15 minutes.
  • Process: Answer questions about your technology's nature and intended use. If your AI system embeds its own general-purpose AI model, you'll be guided into that assessment too.
  • Outcome: A risk classification and a checklist of outstanding vs. fulfilled obligations.

*Note: This assessment is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.
