This questionnaire supports organisations in understanding the maturity of their compliance with regards to AI Act. It will guide you through a short assessment to determine your role (such as provider or deployer), the risk category/classification that applies to your AI system or general-purpose AI model, and the specific obligations that follow from that classification. The questionnaire is an ongoing project, subject to review and update to align with regulatory developments, thus amendments of Digital Omnibus on AI have not yet been incorporated.

  • Duration: 10-15 minutes.
  • Process: Answer questions about your technology's nature and intended use.
  • Outcome: A risk classification and a checklist of outstanding vs. fulfilled obligations based on the answers provided.

For the proper completion of the questionnaire, kindly note the following:

  1. Not all roles covered by AI Act are included in the questionnaire. The roles examined are provider and deployer for AI systems and providers for GPAI models.
  2. In case your entity has more than one roles, repeat questionnaire for each role separately.
  3. The questionnaire should be answered separately for each system or model. If your AI system embeds your own AI model, complete the questionnaire once for the AI system and once for the AI model.
  4. The list of obligations is not exhaustive. Since the questionnaire mainly focuses on the development phase, more obligations can apply eg after the system is marketed or upon requested by the competent authorities.
  5. The questionnaire is adapted to uses cases most likely in the energy sector, thus not all provisions of AI Act (e.g not all cases of prohibited practices or high-risk) are included.
  6. The information provided is based on Consortium's understanding. AI Act provisions may be interpreted differently.
  7. National legislation should be also checked.
